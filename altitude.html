Environment Chamber System

The High Altitude Environment Chamber System at the Singapore Sports Institute (SSI) and National University of Singapore (NUS) is an advanced training and research facility designed to simulate extreme environmental conditions. This system allows athletes to adapt to high-altitude environments and other challenging climates without leaving the training facility.
